PacMan

This is a classic version of pacman built on the p5.js platform. This version features four ghosts with different AIs for chasing down players. The map is based on a double matrix filled with values.

Doodle-Jump

The computer science school of Brown University challenges their freshmen to recreate doodle-jump in Java. I've taken it a step further to make a minimalist Doodle-jump in Javascript.

Classic Baseball

Based on an old, hand-held, highly addictive game of baseball, this game challenges the batter to hit a very fast pitch to score some runs.
